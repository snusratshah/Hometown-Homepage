Visit Quetta

Top three activities to do at Quetta

Enjoy the food in local restaurant

Visit Airport road which is popular for its restaurants.

Go for a hike on Takatu mountain

"Koh-i-Takatu" has different kinds of wild animals living in the mountains.

Visit Hanna Lake and enjoy the cool breeze

Hanna Lake is one of the most visited and accessible lakes in Balochistan.
